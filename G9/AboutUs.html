Misha has grown up in Surrey his whole life, ever since his family moved from Ontario to British Columbia on his sixth birthday. His family was one of board game lovers so it wasn't a suprise when that love of playing games extended to video games when he got his own computer at the age of 12. Just playing on the computer wasn't enough so Misha signed up for a summer camp that taught him about building and upgrading personal computers that same summer. Traditionally an RPG player, misha now enjoys real time strategy games and Magic the Gathering with his friends as well.

Today, Misha still lives in Surrey along with his best friend and their two dog's. His love of computers brought him to BCIT to study in the CST program full time with hopes of one day becoming a sysadmin after graduation. In his spare time he enjoy's food with excessive cheese and going for drives on sunny days.
