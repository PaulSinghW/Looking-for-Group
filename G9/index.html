Welcome to LFG!


This site was created by a team of four software development students as a means for the game lovers of British Columbia to get the latest news about their favorite games and find other game lovers to play them with.

We believe that while games can be fun to play on your own, they are best enjoyed with friends! Many of us have packed schedules that make it hard to go out and make new connections, which is especially harder if you are trying to find other people to play games with.

Our goal is to create a fun and engaging environment for people who live in British Columbia to meet others interested in their favorite games and then give them a medium through which to arrange a place and time to play together. Whether your niche is board games or RPG's, MMO's or FPS's, we want you to feel welcome here in our little community!

Alex, Dasha, Misha & Simmran
